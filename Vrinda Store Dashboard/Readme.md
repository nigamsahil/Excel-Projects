## 📊 Project Insights

1. **Women are the major customers**
   - Women contributed around **64% of total sales**, which shows that female customers are the main target audience for Vrinda Store.

2. **Adult age group has the highest contribution**
   - The **adult age group (30–49 years)** placed the highest number of orders.
   - Adult women are the most valuable customer segment for the store.

3. **Amazon is the top sales channel**
   - Amazon contributed the highest share of orders, around **35.5%**.
   - Myntra and Flipkart are also important platforms for sales growth.

4. **Maharashtra is the top-performing state**
   - Maharashtra generated the highest sales, followed by **Karnataka** and **Uttar Pradesh**.
   - These states can be targeted for future marketing campaigns.

5. **Most orders were successfully delivered**
   - A large percentage of orders were delivered successfully, showing good order fulfillment performance.

6. **Sales are concentrated in limited platforms**
   - Most sales came from Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart.
   - This means the company should focus more on these platforms to increase revenue.

7. **Best target audience**
   - The best customer segment for Vrinda Store is **adult women living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**.

## ✅ Final Recommendation

Vrinda Store should focus more on **adult women customers** by running targeted offers and advertisements on **Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart**, especially in high-performing states like **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**. This strategy can help increase sales and improve business growth.
